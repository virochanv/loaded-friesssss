how to make loaded fries


Ingredients

5 Potatoes

grated cheese

oregano (optional)

basil (optianal)

whatever ingredients you want to add


Utensils

tray

baking paper

potatoe pealer

Instructions

preheat oven to 220 degrees

potatoe pealer to peal all 5 potatoes

slice potatoes into thin peices

place baking paper on the tray and then lay the potatoe chips on the tray

now sprinkle oregano, basil and the grated cheese over the potato chips

you can add whatever ingredients you want to add now

place the tray in the oven for 15 minutes

Now it's ready to eat
